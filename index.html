About Us

As freshmen at Vellore Institute of Technology, Chennai, we are driven by a shared ambition to make a significant impact in the space sector. Our goal is to not only represent our university but also our country, India, at the international level. Despite being new to this journey, our determination knows no bounds. We are committed to mastering the domain of sounding rockets, enhancing our knowledge, skills, and expertise. Together, we aspire to contribute meaningfully to the space sector and leave a lasting mark in the field of exploration and discovery. We believe in the power of collaboration and teamwork, which is why we bring together students from all engineering disciplines to contribute their unique skills and perspectives to our projects. We are dedicated to pushing the boundaries of what is possible in space tech, and we can't wait to see what we will achieve together.
