About Us

We are a passionate group of students from VIT Chennai who came together in March 2023 to form a college rocketry team. As first-year students from various engineering disciplines, we share a common interest in space technology and the thrill of exploring the final frontier. Our mission is to develop and manufacture model rockets that can be launched and operated efficiently. We believe in the power of collaboration and teamwork, which is why we bring together students from all engineering disciplines to contribute their unique skills and perspectives to our projects. We are dedicated to pushing the boundaries of what is possible in space tech, and we can't wait to see what we will achieve together.
